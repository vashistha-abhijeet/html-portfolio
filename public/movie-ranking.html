THE BEST MOVIES ACCORDING TO ABHIJEET

My top 3 movies of all time

Real Steel

Real Steel is a sci-fi action-drama film directed by Shawn Levy and starring Hugh Jackman, Dakota Goyo, and Evangeline Lilly. Set in the near future (around 2020), the movie imagines a world where human boxing has been replaced by robot boxing — massive remote-controlled robots battle in the ring for entertainment and money.

The story follows Charlie Kenton (Hugh Jackman), a former boxer whose career ended when robots took over the sport. Now struggling as a small-time promoter, Charlie scrapes by with outdated robots and risky bets. His life changes when he reconnects with his estranged young son, Max (Dakota Goyo), after the boy’s mother passes away. Together, they discover Atom, an old discarded sparring bot with unique resilience and the ability to mimic human movements.

Against all odds, Charlie and Max train Atom, who starts rising through the ranks of underground and professional robot boxing. Along the journey, father and son rebuild their broken bond. The film blends family drama, underdog sports storytelling, and futuristic action, with thrilling robot fights and emotional depth.

Genre: Sci-Fi, Sports, Action, Drama
Release Date: October 2011
Main Theme: Redemption, father-son relationship, and believing in the underdog.

Transformers

The Transformers movie (2007), directed by Michael Bay, is a sci-fi action film based on the popular Hasbro toy line and animated series.The story follows an ancient war between two factions of alien robots:

Autobots (led by Optimus Prime) – heroic robots who fight to protect humanity.
Decepticons (led by Megatron) – evil robots who seek domination and destruction.

These robots can transform into cars, trucks, jets, and other machines, allowing them to blend into human society. The plot centers on a teenager, Sam Witwicky (Shia LaBeouf), who unknowingly holds the key to locating the powerful artifact called the AllSpark, which can create or destroy worlds. The Autobots and Decepticons battle on Earth to control it, dragging Sam, his love interest Mikaela (Megan Fox), and the U.S. military into the war.

The film is filled with high-energy action, explosive battles, advanced CGI that brought the robots to life, and humor mixed with human drama. It was a box office hit and launched a multi-film franchise.

Avengers

The Avengers (2012) is a Marvel superhero film directed by Joss Whedon. It’s the first big crossover movie in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).

The story follows Nick Fury, the director of S.H.I.E.L.D., as he assembles a team of extraordinary heroes to stop an impending global threat. When Loki, the adopted brother of Thor, steals the Tesseract (a powerful energy source) and plans to use it with an alien army to conquer Earth, Fury recruits Earth’s mightiest heroes:

Iron Man (Tony Stark) - the genius billionaire in high-tech armor
Captain America (Steve Rogers) - the super-soldier from World War II
Thor - the Norse god of thunder
Hulk (Bruce Banner) - the brilliant scientist with uncontrollable strength
Black Widow (Natasha Romanoff) - the skilled spy and assassin
Hawkeye (Clint Barton) - the expert archer and marksman


At first, the team struggles with internal conflicts and egos, but when New York is attacked by Loki’s alien army (the Chitauri), they unite and fight together. The climax showcases the Avengers battling side by side, proving that they truly are Earth’s Mightiest Heroes.
The movie mixes action, humor, teamwork, and epic battles, and it set the stage for many MCU films that followed.
